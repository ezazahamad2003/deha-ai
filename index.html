About Deha AI

The Problem

Overburdened Healthcare

As healthcare systems grow busier, patients lack immediate answers to critical questions.

Limited Resources

Doctors and nurses are overburdened; case managers are expensive or unavailable.

Senior Care Challenges

Seniors especially need ongoing support to manage medications, appointments, and their health journey.

Our Solution

Deha AI provides a personal AI-powered medical case manager that:

  • Provides accurate, 24/7 answers to medical questions
  • Maintains a secure health record vault
  • Sends smart medication & appointment reminders
  • Allows file uploads (prescriptions, tests, etc.) and interprets them
  • Is accessible via voice or chat
